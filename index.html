Our Story

A Culinary Journey
Through Kerala & Beyond

Nestled along Ooty Road at Manathumangalam Bypass, Chillies has been Perinthalmanna's crown jewel of dining since its inception. What began as a humble vision to serve authentic Kerala flavours has grown into the region's most beloved multi-cuisine experience.

From the soulful aromas of Chatti Choru served in traditional clay ware, to the sizzling perfection of our Broasted Chicken and the aromatic layers of our signature Biryanis — every dish is a testament to our commitment to quality, tradition, and bold flavour.
